Beyond the Basics: How NutriWise Stands Out Among Existing Solutions

Background


Poor nutrition affects people of all walks of life. Poor nutrition further harms people's physical health and their mental health as well. A main contributor to poor nutrition is lack of access to healthy foods, yet those that have health options widely available to them rarely partake in healthy eating behaviors due to a lack of knowledge, motivation, and support.

Significance


Due to poor nutrition's affect on people's mental and physical health, interventions aimed at improving nutrition should treat both aspects. Although there are nutrition apps, many of them lack precision care. They are only fixated on counting calories, lack therapeutic approaches to target mental health challenges & behavior change, and have a narrow selection of cultural cuisines. This is why we have developed NutriWise Bot, an app that aims to promote healthier eating habits through a holistic approach.

Our Core Values

Our mission is to improve user's nutrition by using an AI chatbot to make assistance more accessible.

MOTIVATION

By improving user's intrinic and extrinic motivations, users can gain independence from the Bot and apply nutritional knowledge in real life.

CULTURE

Our diverse healthy cuisines from different cultures makes eating health exciting. This makes cuisines more personalized increase user adherence.

HOLISTIC CARE

Beyond only looking at numerical food data, our company aims at healing the user holisitically by improving user's mindset and improving user's intrinsic and extrinsic motivation.

What makes NutriWise Bot different?



With a focus on motivating users to engage in our app, NutrWise Bot acts as your best friend, reminding you to learn about your eating habits. Through the use of customizable characters and journey sagas, you can be rewarded for their improvement. Above that, for longer term effects our Bot applies ACT therapy techniques to health users mentally as well.
